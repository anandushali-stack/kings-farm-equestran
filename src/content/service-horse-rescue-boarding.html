"Livery" is a somewhat outdated term, but it's still in use in some regions today, particularly in Ireland and Great Britain. A livery or livery stable is one that either boards horses for their owners or rents them to the public for riding or driving.

Concierge & Livery Boarding

If you've ever cared for your own horse, you know it's a lot of work. From feeding, grooming and exercising to mucking out its stall, it may seem like the chores never end. Someone else does all this for you when you board your horse out at a livery stable. You show up, you saddle up and you enjoy your horse.
