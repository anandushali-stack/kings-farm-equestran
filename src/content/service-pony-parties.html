Unforgettable Pony Parties: Celebrate with Joy and Adventure at Our Equestrian Oasis! Exclusive Birthday events. Dreamy Pony Parties for All Ages: A Magical Two-Hour Celebration with Ponies, Costumes, and More!

Our team consists of horses and dogs. We provide birthday parties, lessons and camps. Join us for all the fun. You can choose from a unicorn themed party, cowboy, or pony party.

Party with Ponies

We offer Pony Parties for all ages. The party is two hours long with four ponies, costumes, a small bounce house, painting hooves, grooming, painting / decorating the horses. The Farm Manager will also go over safety as well as tell you about predators and prey.

Pony Rides & Fun

You'll get to ride the ponies, learning tricks and loping. Great for birthdays, family functions and reunions.
