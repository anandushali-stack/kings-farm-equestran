Our services

Riding Lessons

We are a fully-equipped, friendly and active club offering riding training that are suitable for riders of all abilities, from complete beginners who have never ridden before up to international competition level. Our training covers dressage, jumping, hacking, with fantastic instructors who enjoy teaching us as much as we like to ride!

02

Show Jumping & Dressage

We are Equestrians – In pursuit of excellence. We specialize in Show Jumping & Dressage and horse training, from schooling movements to competition-level jumping and dressage.

Leadership Training

Learn to communicate effectively, build trust, and foster collaboration within your team through interaction with horses at Kings Equestrian. When it comes to kicking your leadership up a notch, there's nothing quite like a power session with horses.

04

Pony Parties & Picnics

Unforgettable Pony Parties: Celebrate with Joy and Adventure at Our Equestrian Oasis! Exclusive Birthday events. Dreamy Pony Parties for All Ages: A Magical Two-Hour Celebration with Ponies, Costumes, and More!

Horse Rescue & Boarding

Highest quality care for horses; offering everything from boarding and short-term stays, to riding passes and event reservations. Concierge and Livery Boarding services available in Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, and across India.

06

Trails, Safari & Outrides

Escape to Nature: Unforgettable Horseback Riding Adventures. Embark on a unique horseback riding experience at Kings Equestrian. Our extensive trails, spanning over 5 miles, wind through scenic landscapes with wooded hills, babbling creeks, and abundant wildlife.

Equine-Assisted Therapy

Harnessing Healing: A Comprehensive Guide to Equine-Assisted Therapy. Equine Therapy Unveiled: Understanding the Power of Horse-Assisted Healing. Equine-assisted therapy is the all-encompassing term given to any treatment or therapy exercises that incorporate a horse.

Our Club Benefits

Exceptional benefits that set our horse club apart

At Kings Equestrian, riding transcends an activity; it becomes a way of life. Experience the freedom, joy, and profound connection with our horses.

Each rider receives individual attention to grow their confidence, refine their technique, and reach their full potential in a safe and supportive environment.

Kings Equestrian caters to riders of all levels, offering well-trained horses with quality tack for a gratifying and safe riding experience.

Spread over 8 acres, Kings Farm offers a tranquil countryside setting near Bangalore for riding, stabling, training, and breeding of horses.
